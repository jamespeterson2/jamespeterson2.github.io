Ideal Job


Business analyst would be my ideal job for further down in my career as it utilizes the skills I have acquired at university. The position requires people to research the business data to interpret its performance and effectiveness and create visual modelling and predictions alongside strategies that they have come up with to increase its performance to meet the businesses goals. The job requires skills within I.T and an understanding of database management systems to come up with visual models and forecasts for the business to use for performance. all of these skills I have had to use throughout my journey at RMIT university as I have a good understanding of SQL and have had to use it to create visual models many times before. However this job is not obtainable right out of university and it will require me to work jobs at a lower level of management within the I.T side of businesses to obtain adequate experience to be hired for such a high level position.

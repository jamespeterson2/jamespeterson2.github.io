I.T Intrests


My interest in I.T was sparked during my later years in secondary education when I decided to complete a few I.T course that were offered at my school. Ever since then my interest has grown as I have realised how much we all rely on I.T and how much it will continue to grow. Because of this understanding of the vastly expanding I.T industries and a growing reliance on I.T I decided to complete a Bachelor of Business Information Systems at RMIT as this university has both a great business and I.T sector and so learning skills on how to effectively use I.T within the business world be optimal at RMIT.
